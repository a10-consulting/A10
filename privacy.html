Privacy Notice

How we handle inquiries.

A10 Consulting collects the personal information you submit through the contact form, such as your name, email address and message, only to review and respond to your inquiry.

Form submissions are processed through Formspree. Please do not submit confidential, commercially sensitive or regulated information through the form until an appropriate engagement channel is agreed.

We retain inquiry information only for as long as reasonably necessary to respond, manage a potential engagement or comply with legal obligations. You may request access, correction or deletion of your information by contacting A10 Consulting through the website form.

This notice should be reviewed by legal counsel before relying on it as a full GDPR privacy policy.
